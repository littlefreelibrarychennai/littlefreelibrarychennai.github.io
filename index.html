Little Free Library Chennai

TAKE A BOOK, LEAVE A BOOK

Little Free Library Chennai is about 'book sharing', to promote literacy & love for reading along with a sense of'sharing'. The idea is quite simple, take any book but replace it with a different one to be enjoyed by someone else.

Little Free Library Chennai encourages book-sharing and builds community. It provides accessible, convenient and inviting spaces for people to exchange books. This library is open 24/7 and anyone can take a book for free, but we encourage people to donate a book too. We accept all types of books from fiction, to philosophy, marketing, technology, DIY and so much more. Its a great way to connect with our community and share your love of reading. We are proud to say Chennai now has its own free books library.

WHAT'S HAPPENING

Activities and Programs

BOOK COLLECTION DRIVE

Little Free Library Chennai is committed to promoting literacy and the love of reading by providing access to books in communities. We organize book collection drives that allow the community to donate books or have them picked up at their door.

WANT THIS LIBRARY IN YOUR AREA?

Little Free Library Chennai has recently undertaken this. It has been working to make books accessible to everyone by setting up small Libraries across the city. If you like this concept and want to setup one near your home, contact us to get started!

WHAT NEXT

We strive hard to collect books through donations to build an extensive collection of books, periodicals, and a growing variety of books collection.

AVAILABILITY OF BOOKS

We are constantly updating the availability of books, but you play a major role by donating books to us to enable our community of readers. Keep checking often, you might get lucky to find your favourites - for free.

WHAT KEEPS US RUNNING

Family & friends continue to fund as well as source books from other friends & donors. It's important that you do your bit to help us sustain this initiative and to explain this to other areas in the city.

VISION

Little Free Library Chennai is a library with a difference. We provide a community library service to our local area. Our mission is to make books accessible to everyone, no matter their background or circumstances. We also have a unique approach to our library service, with our vision holder Jeyakumar bringing his experience as an Engineer, Wire Tree Sculptor, Numismatist, Philatelist, Antiquarian and Hand Casting Artist to the table.

We believe that knowledge should be shared and celebrated, and our library does just that. Join hands to install more such Little Free Libraries in your communities too.
